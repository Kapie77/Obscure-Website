Name: Josh Carter

Date of birth: November 29th 1985

Description:

Josh Carter is the typical small reporter who spends his leisure time reading science fiction novels and working on his movies. Curious as
a student he is always searching for various scoops and tends to get carried away a little too easily. He always thinks that he is onto the greatest and hottest scoop of the century.
This time he may well be right !

Special Aptitude:

Josh, thanks to his journalist skills, can spot important objects quite easily every time he enters a room (whereas for the others this information can only be collected by looking attentively).
He can also say if anything important is left in a room.
